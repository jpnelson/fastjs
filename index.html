FASTJS.LINK

fastjs.link is a short linking service for npm packages.

Link directly to the home page of npm packages:

  • react.fastjs.link
  • vuejs.fastjs.link
  • express.fastjs.link

fastjs.link reads the homepage field from your latest package.json, and redirects there. Maintain and update your shortlink through npm!

🖥 Open source and open to contribution!
